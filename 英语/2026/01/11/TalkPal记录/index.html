2026/01/11 13:17

  • 一直没弄懂的In/On/At
    • For time, “in” is used for longer periods like months or years, “on” for specific dates, and “at” for precise times.
      • In July/In 2016/In the morning/ On Monday/ On July 31st/ At 5:00PM, At night
    • For location 我没想到竟然是一样的
      • In might be the whole area, On cloud be a specific street, and at is the exact door you are standing by.
        • In London, On the street, at the door
        • In 低（看整体） 像是一个“容器”，你被包裹在其中。 容器、大环境
        • On 中（看表面） 像是一个“平面”或者“线”。 平面、街道、线条
        • At 高（看点） 精确坐标、功能
  • “I would like to…” 是英语中最常用、最稳妥的表达意愿的句式 want to 没有这个礼貌
    • I would like to ask(verb) or sth (a bottle of the water)
